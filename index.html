CCTP Bridge USDC · Sepolia ↔ Arc

Circle's Cross-Chain Transfer Protocol burns USDC on the source chain and mints native USDC on the destination — no wrapped tokens, no liquidity pools.

1
Approve & Burn — TokenMessenger burns your USDC on Sepolia and emits an attestation.
2
Attest — Circle's attestation API signs the burn message (auto-polled every 5s).
3
Mint — MessageTransmitter on Arc Testnet mints native USDC to your wallet.
⚠️ Make sure MetaMask is on Sepolia to initiate a Sepolia → Arc bridge.

Arc Agent Forge

Mint on-chain identities for AI agents · ERC-8004 · Arc Testnet
Connect MetaMask

Every AI agent needs an identity it can prove. ERC-8004 gives agents an on-chain passport: an identity NFT, a reputation ledger, and third-party validation — all on Arc, Circle's USDC-native L1. Forge yours in three clicks. No private keys, no backend — your wallet signs everything.

STEP 1 Forge your agent

Metadata is packed into the token URI itself (data-URI) — no IPFS account needed. Registration mints an ERC-721 identity NFT to your wallet via the official IdentityRegistry.

STEP 2 Your agents

Identity NFTs owned by the connected wallet, read live from ArcScan.

Connect your wallet to load your agents.

STEP 3 Give reputation (optional · needs a second account)

Per ERC-8004, an agent's owner cannot rate their own agent — switch MetaMask to a different account (with a little gas USDC), then record feedback on the ReputationRegistry.
