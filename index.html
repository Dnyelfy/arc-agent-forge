ERC-8183 Create a Job

Agentic Commerce flow — a Client posts a job, a Provider does the work, an Evaluator approves payment. Funds escrow in USDC. For testing, you can be all three roles (set Provider to a 2nd wallet or your agent, Evaluator = you).

🔧 Manage a Job

After creating, the Job ID appears above. Run the lifecycle: set budget → fund (escrow USDC) → submit work → approve to release payment.

STEP 2 — Set budget (Provider or Client)
STEP 3 — Fund / escrow (Client)

Approves & locks the budget in escrow. Job moves to Funded.

STEP 4 — Submit work (Provider)
STEP 5 — Settle (Evaluator)

Approve releases escrow to provider. Reject refunds the client.
